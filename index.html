Welcome to BetaPlus Tutoring

At BetaPlus Tutoring, we offer expert tutoring services to both high school and university students. Whether you're preparing for your HSC, tackling tough university courses, or getting ready to apply for postgraduate study, we’re here to help you succeed.

Our services include:

  • Personalised one-to-one tutoring in mathematics, physics, computing, engineering, and medical physics
  • Small group tutorials for collaborative and cost-effective learning
  • Seminars focused on preparing students for entry into Medical Physics programs in Australia and internationally

Who We Are
